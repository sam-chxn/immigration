Support Our Solution

As a result of physical donations being rejected by CBP at the border, monetary donations are preferred and encouraged if able. By purchasing a S.F.S. bucket hat or backpack, you are supporting the solution through a direct donation to both the KIND and the Save the Children organizations. 100% of the proceeds will be donated to charity.
